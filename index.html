Welcome to Puppyz, a Puppy Adoption Center!

Why adopt a puppy from Puppyz?

Pups Adoption Service gives you the opportunity to adopt a very cute and playful puppy. Our puppies are looking for a family and a home. We make sure to vaccinate and housetrain them before you can take them home. You won't have to potty-train them, yay! Also, we are more affordable than if you were to buy a puppy vs adopt. More savings for you! What are you waiting for? Go find your puppy on our Puppies page.

What breeds of puppies do you have?

All puppies are adorable. We get lots of different breeds but the most popular are:

  • Schnoodles

  • Jack Russell Terriers

  • Australian Shepherds

  • Cavachons

  • Corgis

Where is Puppyz located?

It is located on Mission Street in San Francisco, California - on the corner of Street 45, next to the Park N Buy market. If you get lost, you can call the center's manager called Suzie at (123)456-7890.
